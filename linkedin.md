# Shivam Kotwalia
## GenAI & ML | Ex-(Meta, McKinsey) | GenAI Consultant


### About 
Shivam is a strategy and data leader with 12 years of experience helping businesses drive growth, launch new products, and solve complex operational challenges through analytics, AI, and data-driven decision making. He has partnered closely with CXOs across Fintech, FMCG, Insurance, and Energy sectors to improve commercial performance and scale new business initiatives. He specializes in the practical application of Machine Learning and Generative AI, with experience leading large-scale deployments across multiple industries.

Currently, he leads GenAI, Strategy & Analytics for PhonePe's quick-commerce business, Pincode. Previously, at Tide, (UK-based fintech), he helped build the data foundation for the business and shaped the Savings and Commercial Banking verticals. Prior to that at Meta, he contributed to the launch and growth of Instagram Reels in India, supporting product growth and go-to-market initiatives. At his time at McKinsey -he spent several years advising Fortune 500 organizations on digital transformation, operational efficiency, and AI-driven automation.

Outside of his professional role, Shivam advises early-stage startups on AI strategy and implementation and is passionate about teaching Machine Learning and GenAI. Having trained more than 10,000 students globally, he remains committed to making technology more accessible and actively contributes to the open-source community.
